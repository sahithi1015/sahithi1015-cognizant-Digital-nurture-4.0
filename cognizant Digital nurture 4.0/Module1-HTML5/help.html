Help & Support

Welcome to the help page! Here you'll find answers to common questions and guidance on using the Community Event Portal.

How do I register for an event?

Go to the "Contact" section and fill out the registration form. Make sure all required fields are filled.

How can I find events near me?

Click on the "Find Nearby Events" button in the form section. Make sure your browser allows location access.

Why is the video not playing?

Ensure your browser supports HTML5 video. If the video doesn't load, try refreshing the page.

How do I clear my preferences?

Click the "Clear Preferences" button at the bottom of the form to reset your saved settings.
